Common Questions

Package FAQs

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can start with Standard Starter and upgrade to Growth Booster or Custom Creator as your business grows. We'll work with you to seamlessly transition.

What's included in hosting?

All packages include reliable web hosting with 99.9% uptime, SSL certificate for security, and regular backups to keep your data safe.

How long does it take to build?

Standard Starter typically takes 1-2 weeks, Growth Booster takes 2-3 weeks, and Custom Creator timelines vary based on requirements (usually 3-6 weeks).

Are there any hidden costs?

No hidden costs! Our packages include everything listed. Domain renewal and hosting are billed annually after the first year, and we'll inform you well in advance.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All packages include post-launch support for bug fixes and minor updates. We also offer monthly maintenance plans for ongoing updates and improvements.

Can I see examples of your work?

We'd be happy to share relevant portfolio examples during our consultation. Contact us to discuss your project and see websites we've created for similar businesses.
